Background

Rapid and effective emergency services are provided to the community from four ﬁre stations that are strategically located to allow for the time for arrival from call dispatch of the ﬁrst unit within four to six minutes for medical and ﬁre emergencies and within eight minutes for additional units. Fire Administration and Support Services provide services from their own facilities. Ocean Rescue provides services from one headquarter and three sub-headquarter facilities that coordinate the preventive and rescue efforts of 36 lifeguard towers located to cover all publicly accessible beaches. Emergency Management is run from the Emergency Operations Center at Fire Station #2.
