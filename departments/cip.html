The City established the Office of Capital Improvement Projects in 2001 to serve as a focal point for the planning and construction management activities associated with the extensive and ambitious capital projects being funded by the voter-authorized G.O., Water and Sewer Bond and Stormwater funding at the time. The total value of the Capital Improvement Program has grown from approximately $400 million to over $1 billion. To date, over 200 projects have been completed. The Office of CIP is presently managing over 170 active projects, in various stages from inception through completion.
