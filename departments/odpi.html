Organization Development Performance Initiatives was created to focus on performance excellence by increasing the organization's ability to learn, adapt, and excel within a constantly changing environment, and focus the organization on the customer by aligning the work and performance of team members on the City's identified strategic outcomes.

Mission Statement
"We are dedicated to ensuring our organization implements and sustains strategies that ensure the growth and development of both the organization and our employees to achieve measurable community results."
