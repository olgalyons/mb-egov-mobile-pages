The City of Miami Beach Office of Communications is dedicated to advancing the City's vision through creative and strategic marketing and communications initiatives between its internal and external customers.

The Office of Communications is primarily a link between the City of Miami Beach government and its various departments, the public and the media.

The professional team markets the City's services and programs to its customers in creative ways using various communication tactics to achieve its goals: government-access television, radio, publications, media relations, communication counseling/planning, direct marketing, advertising, Internet, and other telecommunications/new media (ie. List Serve and the use of social marketing sites) methods.
